For our final Visual Computing Systems project we implemented a burst photography pipeline based on Google’s HDR+. 
The technique combines multiple, underexposed raw frames as a means of noise removal, and later applies tone mapping to 
maintain local contrast while brightening shadows. Initially underexposing images allows for more robust alignment and 
merging, in addition to lower motion blur and fewer blown highlights.


对于我们最终的视觉计算系统项目，我们实现了基于 Google HDR+ 的连拍摄影流程。
该技术将多个曝光不足的原始帧组合在一起作为去除噪点的手段，然后应用色调映射以
保持局部对比度，同时使阴影变亮。最初曝光不足的图像可以实现更强大的对齐和合并，
此外还可以降低运动模糊和减少高光。
